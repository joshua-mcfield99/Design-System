Caveat

When to use

Caveat is used for h1-h3 elements

Styling

This font family is used in bold only or 700 if you want the number.

Sample text

M PLUS Rounded 1c

When to use

M PLUS Rounded 1c is used for the bulk of the page i.e, p elements and links. it is also used for some headings to but with a lower priorty over Caveat.

Styling

This font family is used in regular 400 and bold 700. This font family does not have an italic style but if needed most browsers will put the text on a slant if the em tag is used.

Sample text

"The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog"
